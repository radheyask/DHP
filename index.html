About DHP

History

Our company, Durham house of pets which is initially based in New Hampshire is dedicated to providing pet owner’s residing in New Hampshire with the highest quality products to meet the fitness, health and beauty needs of our precious pets. We are currently operating in-house and have recently launched delivery services for most of our products. The company was founded in 2010 due to increased demand for dog food in the area and a lack of production. We started out of the garage of our founder on 11 Garrison avenue and slowly through kick starter campaigns and fundraisers raised enough money to start our first store on Durham main street next to Hannaford Supermarket. We then started partnering with different pet brands and products to diversify our portfolio which led to the growth we see today with over 4 branches all over New Hampshire.

Why use us

Our stores are extremely customer friendly and ofcourse very pet friendly too, you can be guaranteed that you’ll be greeted with a smile as soon as you walk through our doors. We have over 30 years of experience on our staff along with college graduates who are educated in the pet and veterinary industry. We have partnered with the top companies in the nation who have agreed to license out their products to us as well as our very own line of products that we have made after careful research and development to best suit your pet’s needs. We are located at a very convenient location in Durham right next to the supermarket so you can see us while you are walking your dog.
